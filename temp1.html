Yeezy 700

The Adidas Yeezy Boost 700 is the official name for Kanye West's new Adidas Yeezy running shoe. It debuted during the Yeezy Season 5 fashion show in 2017. The shoe released on August 12, 2017 for $300, exclusively via Yeezy Supply. At the time, two colorways of white/gum and black were also expected to release in 2018. The shoe itself has faced large amounts of controversy due to its appearance.
